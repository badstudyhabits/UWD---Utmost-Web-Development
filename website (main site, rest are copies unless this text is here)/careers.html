Careers

Here at UWD, we take pride in what we do, and we appreciate that you decided to apply for us. Below is a form, benefits, and a list of requirements necessary for our open positions.

Benefits

As an emplyee, you get access to multiple benefits within our organisation. For example:

  • Sick Pay
  • Physical and Mental Health Support
  • Lack of lemons in your day

Open Positions:

Junior Developer

You will be:
  • Creating and managing bespoke websites for clients
  • Working closely with our team to discuss future and ongoing projects
  • Communicating with clients to keep them updates on their website
  • Testing websites
Requirements and Qualifications:
  • BTEC Level 3 (or equivelent) in Information Technology
  • Grade 4 (or above) in GCSE English/Mathmatics
  • Experience in HTML/CSS/JavaScript (if not, training and courses can be provided here and on site
  • Ability to collaborate and communicate with others
  • Keen eye for detail
  • Communication skills

Senior Developer

You will be:
  • Working with Junior Developers to create and manage websites
  • Communicating with Project Overseers
  • Optimising websites and other applications
  • Working on the backend of websites
  • Assisting with Placement Students
Requirements and Qualifications:
  • BTEC Level 3 (or equivelent) in Information Technology
  • Grade 5 (or above) in GCSE English/Mathmatics
  • Experience in HTML/CSS/JavaScript/PHP (if not, training and courses can be provided here and on site
  • Ability to collaborate and communicate with others
  • Keen eye for detail
  • Communication skills
  • Leadership skills

Project Overseer

You will be:
  • Working closely with our management team
  • Overseeing website development
  • Optimising websites and other applications
  • Working on the backend of websites
Requirements and Qualifications:
  • BTEC Level 3 (or equivelent) in Business or Information Technology
  • Grade 5 (or above) in GCSE English/Mathmatics
  • Experience in managing groups of individuals
  • Ability to collaborate and communicate with others
  • Keen eye for detail
  • Communication skills
  • Leadership skills

Placement Student

You will be:
  • Learning the ropes of HTML/CSS/JavaScript from our Senior Developers
  • Maintaining the front end of websites
  • Optimising websites to fulfil the requirements of the user
  • Gaining the confidence and skillsets needed to go into part-time or full-time employment
Requirements and Qualifications:
  • Studying a BTEC Level 2/3 (or equivelent) in Information Technology or Software Design & Development
  • Grades 1-4 in GCSE English/Maths
  • Willingness to work with others in a team
